Updating Using the Adjust % or Flat Rate Tool

The Adjust % or Flat Rate tool is used to apply a flat amount or a percentage increase or decrease to one or all the funding lines. The tool is displayed only if you are in the BC Processer role in the organization review hierarchy, the funding line has a base budget in CSF, and the associated document is at your level, giving you edit access.
