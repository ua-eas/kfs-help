General Ledger Correction Process

 >  >  >  >

The G/L Correction Process (GLCP) document is used by central administration to correct errors that occur during G/L processing. The GLCP document allows users to correct errors in several ways.

Initiation of the GLCP document is restricted to members of the KFS-SYS Operations role.
