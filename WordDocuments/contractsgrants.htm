Contracts & Grants

The Contracts & Grants module of the Kuali Financial System (KFS) supports all post-award financial requirements for contracts and grants, including indirect costs and cost share as well as budgets and project to date balances.

   In order to work efficiently in the system's Contracts & Grants screens, you need to understand the basics of the KFS user interface. For information and instructions on logging on and off, navigating, understanding the components of screens, and performing basic operations in the screens, see Overview.
