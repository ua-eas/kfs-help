Capital Asset Management > Capital Asset Management Transaction Documents > Pre-Asset Tagging

Example

 

The Chemistry Department received an asset prior to the invoice being processed. Using pre-asset tagging, the barcode tag is assigned to the PO, then printed and applied to the asset. When the invoice is finally processed against the PO, CAB matches the tag number with the invoice to create the asset in the property system.
