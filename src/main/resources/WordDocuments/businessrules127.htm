Business Rules

•      Exactly one of the following groups of fields must be completed on the Assignee / Delegation tab:

o  Principal Name

o  Namespace and Role Name

o  Group Namespace and Group

•      When delegating an Accounting Reviewer role, the values for the From Amount and To Amount must fall within the range established for the role member whose responsibility is being delegated.
