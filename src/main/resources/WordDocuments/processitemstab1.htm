Process Items Tab

 

The Process Items tab identifies the lines of a specified purchase order or payment request that this credit relates to. It also indicates the dollar amounts to be credited to individual lines and allows you to edit the accounting information ass1ociated with these lines. It also allows you to enter additional credits pertaining to items such as restocking fees or miscellaneous credits.

 

The tab includes two sections: Items and Additional Charges. In the example below, the credit references a purchase order.
