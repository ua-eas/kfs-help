Allocating Charges

 

To allocate additional charges that have not been allocated in a batch process or to allocate a line item to other line items:

1.    In the Active Line Items tab, click the Select box for each item you want to allocate. If more than one line is selected, charges are allocated proportionately.

2.    Click the Allocate button on the line you are allocating the charges from.
