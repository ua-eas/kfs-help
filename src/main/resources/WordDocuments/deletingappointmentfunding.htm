Deleting Appointment Funding

 

The delete button is available when the Salary line is editable and is not already marked delete. To delete appointment funding:

 

Click the Delete button. The position is marked as deleted and the delete button toggles to undelete.

 

Click the Revert or Undelete buttons to cancel the operation.
