Capital Asset Modules > Capital Asset Builder (CAB) > CAB Lookup and Maintenance E-Docs > Pre-Asset Tagging
Example

The Chemistry Department received an asset prior to the invoice being processed. Using pre-asset tagging, the barcode tag is assigned to the PO, then printed and applied to the asset. When the invoice is finally processed against the PO, the CAB module matches the tag number with the invoice to create the asset in the property system.
