Business Rules

     For information about business rules that apply to all endowment transaction e-docs, see Standard Business Rules.

In addition to the standard business rules for endowment transactions, the following business rules apply to holding tax lot rebalance transactions:

•      You may edit the number of units in each tax lot. The total number of units before the change must equal the total number of units after the change.

•      You may edit the carry value in each tax lot. The total carry value before the change must equal the total carry value after the change.

•      Only positive values may be entered.

•      You may bring the value of units and carry value to zero in a tax lot, but you cannot create a new tax lot with the e-doc.

•      If you make one of the values zero, both MUST be zero (that is, if the units are zero, the carry value must also be zero and vice versa).
