New Corporate Credit Card or External Agency Accounts Section

       Your Credit Cards or Travel Agencies can be manually added to a traveler’s TEM Profile by a TEM Profile Administrator or added when the CTS Card Application or Corp Card Application are completed.

Administrator tab, New Corporate Credit Card or External Agency Accounts section definition

Title

Description

Credit Card or Agency Name

Required if any information is entered in this tab. The code that identifies the Credit Card or Travel Agency. Use the lookup  to find the name

Account Number

Required if any information is entered in this tab. Enter the 16 digit credit card or agency account number. The system masks the first 12 characters for all roles except the users in the TEM Profile Administrator role and the traveler.

Expiration Date

Enter the date on which the account is to expire or select it from the calendar .  

Effective Date

Enter the date on which the account is to become effective or select it from the calendar .  

    If this record was added as a result of a CTS Card Application or Corporate Card Application, the effective date will be the day the document was approved.

Note

Enter additional information as desired.

    If this record was added as a result of a CTS Card Application or Corporate Card Application, the note will list the day the document was approved and the document number.

Active

Indicates whether this account information record is active (Y) or inactive (N).
