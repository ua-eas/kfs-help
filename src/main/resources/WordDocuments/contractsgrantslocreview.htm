Contracts & Grants LOC Review

 >  >  >  >

Presents a means for reviewing draw amounts for letter of credit awards, prior to creating contracts and grants invoices and payment transactions for those awards.

    This document is available when Contracts & Grants Billing is turned on.

When you select Contracts & Grants Letter of Credit Review from the Main Menu, the system displays the Contracts & Grants LOC Review Initiation tab.
