Caveats about using this documentation

This documentation is specific to the most recent release of out-of-the box version of Kuali Financials, and this out-of-the-box version and your institutions version of the system may differ in important ways. Consequently, this documentation will not necessarily accurately reflect what you see on your screens, the way your institution uses Financials, or your institutions unique configuration of it.
