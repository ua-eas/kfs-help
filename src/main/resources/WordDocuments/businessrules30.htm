Business Rules

•      Fields marked required must be filled in.

•      The CSF Delete Code entry can be null. The implementing institution must implement the validation for an appropriate value to mean delete.

•      An active/inactive flag is used to indicate to the CSF Override process to apply active overrides only.

•      The Fiscal Year entry must be the current fiscal year.

•      The appointment must be valid, as defined by your institution.

•      The position must be valid, as defined by your institution.
