Year-End Document Routing

 

Year-End documents route differently from their regular counterpart documents. Most notably they skip the fiscal officer level of routing and go directly to Organization Review. They will also optionally route based on sub-fund and CG Responsibility ID used on Contracts & Grants account.

 

      Organization Review must be set up for Year End documents or they will route to exception.
