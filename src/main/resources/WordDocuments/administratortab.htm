Administrator Tab

The Administrator tab associates one or more corporate credit cards and/or travel agencies with the traveler and displays the AR Customer ID if the traveler has one.

     Only users in the Travel Profile Administrator role may edit information in this tab.

The tab contains two sections— New Corporate Credit Card and External Agency Accounts and Profile Status.
