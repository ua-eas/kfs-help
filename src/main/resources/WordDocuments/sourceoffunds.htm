Source of Funds

The Source of Funds is a field on the Account Details tab that specifies the primary funding source (e.g. Federal, State, Private) for each account.  This field enhances reporting by providing a mechanism to aggregate data by funding source.

 

When the parameter, DISPLAY_SOURCE_OF_FUNDS_IND = Y, the Source of Funds field will display on  the account inquiry screen and the account details tab.
