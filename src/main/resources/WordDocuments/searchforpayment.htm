Search for Payment

 >  >  >

The Search for Payment lookup allows you to find individual payments in the PDP by searching on a variety of search criteria. Search results display identifying information including the disbursement date, type, status and number. You may select individual payments from the search results to view additional detail information.

The Search for Payment screen is restricted to users with the KFS-PDP Customer Contact, KFS-PDP Manager or KFS-PDP Processor roles.
