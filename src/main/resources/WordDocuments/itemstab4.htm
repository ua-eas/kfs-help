Items Tab

The Items tab identifies what is being ordered on this requisition and establishes an accounting distribution indicating how those items should be charged. It allows multiple items and accounting distributions to be added on a single requisition.

The Items tab includes four sections: Add Item, Current Items, Additional Charges, and Totals.
