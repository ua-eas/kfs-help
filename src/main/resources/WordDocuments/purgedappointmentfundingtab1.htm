Purged Appointment Funding Tab

A funding line can be purged only if no associated CSF record exists. A subsequent save operation permanently purges this account’s funding for this position and the incumbent when the budget is loaded into the general ledger and CSF Tracker. The purged funding lines are moved to the Purge Appointment tab and can be restored by clicking the restore button up until a save operation is performed.
