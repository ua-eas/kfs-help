Creating a TEM Profile

     Only the traveler and users in the TEM Travel Administrator role may create TEM Profiles.

The procedure for creating a TEM Profile varies, depending on whether you are creating your own TEM Profile or the Profile Administrator is creating a TEM Profile for an employee or a non-employee.
