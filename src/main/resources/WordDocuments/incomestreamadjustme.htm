Income Stream Adjustments

 

If a Budget Adjustment involves two or more accounts with different Income Stream Accounts, Kuali Financials can be configured to create an automated Transfer of Funds transaction to properly adjust cash between those Income Stream accounts. This Transfer of Funds is generated by Kuali Financials after the Budget Adjustment is approved and does not route for approval itself.
