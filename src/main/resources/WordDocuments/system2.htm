System maintenance documents provide lists of valid system-wide attributes including banks, buildings, origination codes, rooms, tax regions, tax region types, units of measure, and the mapping of various university dates to fiscal years and fiscal periods.

All users may look up valid values in these documents. Users with the appropriate role may copy, edit and create new values through these documents.
