Initiating a POPH Document

 

1.    Search for the PO from the PO search screen and open the PO.

2.    Click Payment Hold button.

3.    Enter the reason for the hold.

4.    Click Yes button.

5.    Clicklock button when you see the confirmation message.

The system displays the dashboard.
