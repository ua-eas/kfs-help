Document Layout

The Proposal/Award Close document includes the Close tab. This tab includes Perform Close and Close Information sections.

Date Closed

Enter the date the close process should take place or select it from the calendar .

Close on or before

Enter the date chosen as the threshold for closing proposals and awards or select it from the calendar .

When the close process runs all proposals with a Submission Date and without a Close Date and all awards with an Entry Date and without a Close Date is closed if their Submission/Entry date is equal to or earlier than this date.

Title

Description

Display-only. Displays the number of Proposals closed by the last close process.
