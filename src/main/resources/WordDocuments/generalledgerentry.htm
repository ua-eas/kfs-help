General Ledger Entry

 

The General Ledger Entry inquiry displays the individual transactions that make up the balance in an account. It is the same inquiry that can be displayed by drilling down into the General Ledger Balance inquiry. You must enter either the fiscal year, chart, and account number or the document number.

 

The drilldown pattern of this inquiry is as follows:

1.    General Ledger Entry

2.    Financials Document of Labor Ledger Entries
