Sufficient Funds Rebuilder Report

After the sufficientFundsRebuilderJob is complete, a report is created and placed in the reports/gl directory. The report includes a detailed listing of sufficient funds errors (if any) and a summary of records read, deleted, converted or retained due to errors by the sufficient funds rebuilder process. Also records the number of entries added, deleted and updated in the sufficient funds table. Total and record count of Sufficient Balance Table rows selected, deleted, updated, and inserted.
