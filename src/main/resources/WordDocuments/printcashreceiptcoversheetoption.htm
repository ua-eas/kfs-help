Print Cash Receipt Cover Sheet Option

If you need to send a paper copy of the CR document to the verifying unit along with the accompanying media (cash, checks, coins, etc.) you can print a PDF version of a cash receipt by clicking the Print Cash Receipt Coversheet linkwhich appears in the top middle area of the document after submitting the document.
