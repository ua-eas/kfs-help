Business Rules

 

•      Total effort must equal 100%.

•      An effort distribution line involving an account that is now closed is not editable.

•      Existing effort distribution lines cannot be deleted.

•      Salary amounts cannot be negative.

•      Salary lines can only be changed up to a half percent of the total salary amount for this effort report.

•      Salary can only be modified after a change to effort.

•      When adding a new line there must not be an existing line with the same chart, account, and sub-account.

•      Total salary amount cannot change by more than .009 (rounding).
