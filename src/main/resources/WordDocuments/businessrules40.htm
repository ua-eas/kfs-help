Business Rules

•      Three conditions must be present to initiate the Equipment Loan/Return document for the purpose of recording a return:

o  The asset must exist in the database, and

o  the tag must be attached to the equipment, and

o  the tag number must be entered in the Asset document.

•      The value in the Last Inventory Date field will automatically be updated to match that of the Loan Date field.

•      The asset return link is available in the Actions column only when this asset has previously been lent.
