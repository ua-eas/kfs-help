Organization

 > >>> >

The Organization document defines the entities representing units at many different levels within the institution (for example, a department, responsibility center, campus). The document collects important identifying information about an Organization and defines its place in the organization hierarchy. Organization is an important part of the Chart of Accounts and the organization hierarchy is frequently leveraged by the KFS for use in Workflow and reporting.
