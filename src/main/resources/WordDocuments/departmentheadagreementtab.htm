Department Head Agreement Tab

The Department Head Agreement tab allows the Fiscal Officer to signify their agreement with the responsibilities that come with having a corporate card issued to a person in their department.

 

The tab has two components:

•      The agreements related to the having a corporate card issued to a person in the Fiscal Officer’s department.

•      An Accept Department Head Agreement check box. In order to approve this document, this box must be checked.
