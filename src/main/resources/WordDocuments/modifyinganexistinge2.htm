Modifying an Existing Effort Distribution line in the Effort Summary Section

To modify an existing effort distribution line in the Effort Summary or Effort Detail section, follow the steps below:

1.     Enter the new effort percentage in the Effort field.

2.     Click the recalculate button.

The system recalculates the value in the Salary field in all totals.

3.     If necessary, make minor modifications to the Salary field.

       Remember that salary modifications cannot exceed .009 of the total salary on the document and no one salary distribution line can be modified by more than .005 of the total salary.

4.     In the Notes and Attachments tab, type a note explaining the change and click the Add button.

5.     Click the  button.
