Editing a Building Address

 

The address is completely editable before the PO is approved. Take the following steps to add an address for another building.

1.    Click the Building Not Found button.

The delivery address fields become editable.

2.    Enter the delivery address.

     To search for a delivery address, click the search icon next to Building and select a building from the lookup.
