Additional Inquiry Fields

 

Two additional fields appear on the Payee ACH Account Inquiry screen that don’t appear on the document.

 

Payee ACH Account Inquiry additional field definitions

Title

Description

Auto-Inactivation Indicator

Will be set to Yes if the record is inactive and was made so by the Payee ACH Auto-Inactivation job. This job looks for inactive person or vendor records and inactivates any corresponding Payee ACH Account records. Will be set to No is the record is still active or was made inactive via a document as opposed to through the auto-inactivation job.

Last Update Time

Date and timestamp for when this record was last modified.
