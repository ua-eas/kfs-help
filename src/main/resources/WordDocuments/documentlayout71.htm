Document Layout

 

The document layout of the PORH document is identical to that of the original PO, with an additional notation regarding the status in the Document Header. A notation also displays at the top of the PO document, such as 'Warning: There is a pending action on this Purchase Order.'

 

      For information about the fields on this document, see Document Layout under Purchase Order.
