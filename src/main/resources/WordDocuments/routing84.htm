Routing

The electronic invoice batch job creates an EIRT document upon unsuccessful matching to a purchase order and routes the document to members in the KFS-PURAP_Accounts Payable Processor action list. Your institution may also configure routing rules based on reject reasons.

At AP approval, the document status is 'Final'. At AP disapproval, the document status is 'Disapproved'.
