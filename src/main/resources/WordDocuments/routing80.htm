Travel, Entertainment and Moving > Standard Transaction Documents > Travel Arranger > Process Overview
Routing

•      When you use this document to become a travel arranger for someone, the system routes it to the traveler for approval. After approval, the status of the document becomes Final and the system adds the arranger to the Arranger tab in the traveler’s TEM Profile record.

•      When you use this document to resign from this relationship with a traveler, the status of the document becomes Final immediately. The system sends an FYI to the traveler and marks your Arranger record as inactive on the TEM Profile.
