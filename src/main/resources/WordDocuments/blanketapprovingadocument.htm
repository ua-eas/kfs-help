Blanket Approving a Document

If you are a member of a role with a blanket approve document permission, you have the option to blanket approve a document routed to you for your approval.

Note that you can only blanket approve a document you are initiating or a document for which you already have an approval request.

Click Blanket Approve button to approve the document bypassing all other approvals.
