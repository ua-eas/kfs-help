Accessing CAB Functions

The Main Menu tab offers access to all user-initiated CAB functions in the KFS.

•      From the Administrative Transactions submenu, users may access transactions fed from the General Ledger and the Accounts Payable modules and work with them.

•      From the Lookup and Maintenance submenu, users may access the Pre-Asset Tagging function.
