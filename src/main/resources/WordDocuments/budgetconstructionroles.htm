Budget Construction Roles

Access to the Budget Construction documents is restricted. The following users are allowed to perform Budget Construction activities as indicated.

•      The fiscal officer or delegate (for the BC document type or the special value 'ALL') may prepare budgets for his/her accounts when the associated document is at level zero.

•      Users in the BC Processors role may pull up the BC document up to their own organization level and push it down to any level in the hierarchy below their approval level.

•      The BC processor at the top level of the organization hierarchy may unlock the document and import pay rates.
