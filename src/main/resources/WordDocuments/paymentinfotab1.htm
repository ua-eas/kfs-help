Payment Info Tab 

If recurring payments are required for the items on this PO, this tab is used to indicate the type of payment schedule required and the duration of that schedule. The fields in this tab are informational only. They do not drive any other functions within the KFS.

The Payment Info tab includes Payment Info and Billing Address sections.
